The Process

How It Works

01

Apply & Consult

A private application followed by a one-on-one consultation with our team to understand who you are and who you're looking for.

02

Review the Bio

A written profile is shared with no names and no photos. Both parties must approve before anything moves forward.

03

Photos Exchanged

Photos are shared privately, and only after both parties have approved the written bio. Substance first, always.

04

He Plans the Date

The gentleman receives contact information and is expected to arrange a proper first date — promptly and thoughtfully.
